Full Name: Homer Jay Simpson

He is a crude, ignorant, and slobbish individual, although he is fundamentally a good person and shows great caring and loyalty to his family, friends and on occasion, to those he is barely knows or those he considers his enemies. Homer works as a low-level safety inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant in Sector 7G, although he is often incompetent and negligent towards his duties.

Voice Actor: Dan Castellaneta
