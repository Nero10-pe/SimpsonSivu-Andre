Full Name: Bartholomew Jojo "Bart" Simpson

Is the oldest child of the Simpson family and the deuteragonist in The Simpsons. He is the only son of Homer and Marge Simpson, and the older brother of Lisa and Maggie. He is mischievous, rebellious, misunderstood, disruptive and "potentially dangerous."

Voice Actor: Nancy Cartwright
